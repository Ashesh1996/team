Plan of Work

This web application allows registered NGO to get information about the waste food of any organization/party/restaurants. Any NGO or other users can register in our web application to take part in the Mission- “Save Food Stop Wastage”. When there will unused excess food in any party or restaurants or home, the registered users will post the details and based on their locations and food quantity, a notification will be send over the nearby registered NGO for picking the food such that no poor people stays hungry. We also do proper analysis regarding percentage of food wastage within an area, which not only aware people from food wastage, but provides the success rate feeding poor hungry people.
